About the Logo

Cosmos is defined as the universe seen as a well-ordered whole.

The logo resembles a comet -- a part of the universe that makes it whole.

A comet might look very small from afar but by the time it lands to the Earth it creates a huge impact and leaves a big mark. A mark that will always be remembered through history.

Each member of StraightArrow’s COSMOS team is a comet. They are comets that shine the brightest when they are together. Comets that will make SA continue leaving huge beautiful marks in the world, or the universe rather.
